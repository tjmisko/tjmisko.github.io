Who we are

We're a group of UC Berkeley students and community-members who love swing dancing! Join us for FREE weekly beginner and intermediate swing dance lessons. All you need is to bring yourself and a pair of comfy shoes to dance in. No partner or dance experience is required.

When we meet

11:45 AM to 2:30 PM every Saturday, rain, shine, or midterms! We generally continue meeting over campus breaks in the winter and and summer as well.

Where we meet

Upper Sproul Plaza on the Campus of UC Berkeley, right in front of Golden Bear Cafe.

COVID-19 Safety

Social dancing is an inherently risky activity in the midst of a pandemic. We at Lindy on Sproul would like attendees to make informed decisions about risk. We require full vaccination of all attendees and strongly encourage masking during social dancing, but these measures do not guarantee safety.

Please attend responsibly. Stay home if you are feeling sick or have tested positive recently, and be mindful of those in your social orbit. If you are, are leaving with, or are in regular close contact with someone who is at high risk, you should exercise caution in attending social dancing events

Customarily, our lessons involve the rotation of partners. If you are uncomfortable rotating, it is OK to bring an exclusive partner or to dance within a bubble, however this is by no means required.
