* Led development of the SSPI, a policy index scoring and ranking national policies across Sustainability, Market Structure, and Public Goods for 49 countries
* Led several teams of undergraduates in data collection and policy research to engineer over 50 data-driven policy indicators
* Conducted sensitivity testing to ensure the robustness of indicators and aggregation methodology
* Assessed the extent to which the SSPI policy index tracks performance metrics using regression analysis
